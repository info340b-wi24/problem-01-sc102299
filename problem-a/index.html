Hua Mulan

Hua Mulan is a legendary figure from ancient China. She is most famously depicted in the "Ballad of Mulan," a Chinese poem. Also, Mulan is characterized as a courageous and skilled warrior who disguises herself as a man to take her ailing father's place in the army. There is more information: Ballad of Mulan.

The reason I Admire Hua Mulan

  • She is beautiful.
  • She challenged traditional gender roles.
  • She was highly resilient and adaptable in face of adversity.
